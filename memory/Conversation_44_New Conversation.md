Here is a component list for building a drone:

1.  **Frame:** The structural chassis.
2.  **Brushless Motors:** Provide thrust; quantity depends on drone type (e.g., 4 for a quadcopter).
3.  **Electronic Speed Controllers (ESCs):** Control motor speed based on flight controller commands.
4.  **Propellers:** Convert motor rotation into lift.
5.  **Flight Controller (FC):** The central processing unit, interprets commands and stabilizes the drone.
6.  **Radio Receiver (Rx):** Receives control signals from the remote transmitter.
